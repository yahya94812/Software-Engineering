## ğŸ” **What is a License?**

A license is a legal document you include in your repository (usually as a `LICENSE` file) that tells others:

* Can they **use** your code?
    
* Can they **modify** it?
    
* Can they **redistribute** it?
    
* Can they **use it commercially**?
    
* Do they need to **give credit**?
    
* Do they need to **open-source** their changes?
